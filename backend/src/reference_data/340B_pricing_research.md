# 340B / Public Pricing Research — 33 Oncology Drugs

## Why exact 340B ceiling prices are not shown per drug

The **actual** 340B ceiling price for every NDC is published only inside HRSA's official 340B OPAIS Ceiling Price database, which requires an authorized covered-entity or manufacturer login — it is **not publicly downloadable**.
Source: [HRSA 340B Ceiling Price Lookup](https://340bpricing.hrsa.gov/Help/CoveredEntity/Ceiling%20Price%20Lookup/NDC%20Product%20Details.htm)

Statutory formula (42 CFR Part 10, Subpart B): 340B ceiling price = AMP − URA (Unit Rebate Amount), with a mandatory minimum 23.1% discount off AMP for most drugs (17.1% for pediatric/generic), further reduced by CPI-penalty inflation adjustments.
Source: [eCFR 42 CFR Part 10 Subpart B](https://www.ecfr.gov/current/title-42/chapter-I/subchapter-A/part-10/subpart-B)

Independently published hospital-transparency research (Community Oncology Alliance) finds average 340B acquisition cost runs **~27% below Medicare ASP-based reimbursement**, with hospitals billing payers at markups reported up to **190–300%+** of acquisition cost.
Source: [COA 340B Hospital Transparency Report](https://communityoncology.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/COA_340B_hospital_transparency_report_2_final.pdf)

Below: every figure is a public benchmark actually retrieved via search or direct source verification (WAC/list price, or cash price — labeled per row). None is the true confidential 340B ceiling price; none is estimated or inferred.
